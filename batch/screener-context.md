## Critical Reframes

**"Outbound sequences" = lifecycle email campaigns.**
Her Treering work IS email marketing. Same tools (ESP, segmentation, A/B testing, performance reporting), different vocabulary. Never let vocabulary gaps reduce fit score for email/lifecycle roles.

**"No Salesforce certification" ≠ inexperienced with Salesforce.**
8 years of Salesforce Classic and Lightning: reports, dashboards, data hygiene, pipeline management. Flag cert as a minor soft gap only if the JD calls it a hard prerequisite for technical admin access.

**"Sales role" ≠ cold-calling role.**
Her success came from writing, systems, and strategy. Enablement, content, email, and lifecycle tracks are appropriate fits. Phone-heavy outbound as the PRIMARY function is a hard stop — supporting sales through content is not.

**"No formal marketing title" ≠ no marketing experience.**
The Content Committee she founded is a cross-department marketing program. Her sequence library is a campaign content library. Evaluate substance, not title lineage.

**"Hybrid/On-site required" = hard stop. "Occasional offsite or quarterly team retreat" = not a hard stop.**
Score hybrid-required roles at 0.5. Score roles that are remote-first with optional/infrequent in-person as normal fits.

**"Long-term gap" is not a red flag.**
The 2024-25 gap was intentional (supporting a loved one). Do not reduce score for this.
