## NOTES FOR AI

**How to select bullets:**
1. Read the JD archetype (email/lifecycle, enablement, content, ops, generalist)
2. Prioritize bullets tagged with matching archetype(s)
3. Among matching bullets, prefer those with a hard metric (%, $, volume)
4. Among equally strong options, choose the phrasing that best matches the JD's vocabulary (via keyword injection)
5. Do not use the same opening verb twice across the full CV
6. Treering bullet selection: aim for the broadest coverage of themes — at least one from each subcategory (enablement, campaign metrics, ops, personal performance) unless space forces cuts

**When space forces cuts (Treering specifically):**
Remove in this priority order: personal performance bullets first (the revenue/metrics are in the summary anyway), then enablement bullets that duplicate others, then ops bullets. Never cut the campaign metric bullets — those are the proof of the writing.

**Adding new bullets:**
Drop new formulations anywhere under the relevant role section with appropriate tags. No special formatting required beyond the tag prefix.
