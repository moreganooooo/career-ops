## Critical Context Notes

### Early SDR Call Model
When the SDR team launched, AEs (Brady, TJ, Kris, Kristie) ran demos — but Morgan BOOKED every
meeting through cold outreach and joined calls muted. Every call note in Morgan's Drive = a meeting
she sourced. "Me:" in notes = Morgan ran the call solo (stronger claim).

### Sequence Authorship Split
Kristina (VP Marketing) tried to take over all sequence writing. Morgan negotiated to keep niche
segments. **Key proof:** Summer '20 Sequence List (TR-0208): 21 Morgan-authored / 18 Kristina —
unambiguous column-by-column attribution. Morgan also proofed ALL 39 sequences including Kristina's 18.

**Metric caveat:** Niche sequence metrics (Private, Title 1) may be elevated — reps added
non-niche prospects to them. Note this when citing engagement rates for those sequences.

### SalesLoft Attribution
Many SalesLoft SUMMARY screenshots show "by morgan@treering.com" verbatim. This is direct
tool-sourced attribution — use this exact phrasing in career docs.
