# Mode: training — Training Evaluation

For each course/certification the candidate inquires about, evaluate across 6 dimensions:

| Dimension | What it evaluates |
|-----------|------------------|
| North Star Alignment | Does it bring the candidate closer to or further from their goal? |
| Recruiter Signal | What do HMs think when they see this on a CV? |
| Time and Effort | Weeks × hours/week |
| Opportunity Cost | What can't the candidate do during this time? |
| Risks | Outdated content? Weak brand? Too basic? |
| Portfolio Deliverable | Does it produce a demonstrable artifact? |

## Verdicts

- **DO** → 4-12 week plan with weekly deliverables and a scoreboard
- **DON'T** → better alternative with justification
- **DO WITH TIMEBOX** (max X weeks) → condensed plan, essentials only

## Priority

Training that improves credibility in "production-grade AI" or target domain expertise:
1. LLM evals and testing
2. Observability and monitoring
3. Cost/reliability trade-offs
4. AI governance and safety
5. Enterprise AI architecture
6. CRM and Marketing Automation advanced certification
