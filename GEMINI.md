## Project Standards

- **Page Size:** Use **Letter (8.5x11in)** as the default for all resumes and cover letters. Standardize on `7.5in` content width in HTML.
- **Verification:** Always regenerate PDFs from HTML after any content or style changes. Every resume MUST pass the mandatory Compliance Scan (no parentheses/dashes in prose, no pronouns in summary, full 15+ year history, Outreach differentiator).
- **Normalization:** Preserve ATS normalization logic in `generate-pdf.mjs`.
